Application · Marefu · Thirty nights · Twelve seats

Twelve questions.

Not a vetting. A conversation in writing. We read every one personally and reply within seven days. There are no wrong answers. Roughly twenty minutes.

Question one of twelve
Your name, as you would like us to use it.
Question two
Where we can write back.
Question three
Where do you live, and how long have you lived there?
Question four
What do you do with most of your days at the moment?
Paid work, unpaid work, craft, care. Whatever "doing" has meant for you recently.
Question five
What is your relationship to East Africa?
Home. Lineage. Longing. Curiosity. None of the above. Any answer is welcome.
Question six
When did you last take more than two weeks off from work in a row?
Question seven
Tell us about a season you remember well.
A stretch of time in your life — weeks or months — that felt like it had its own weather. What made it one.
Question eight
Who, if anyone, would you travel with?
Partner, friend, sibling, no one. We do not hold seats in pairs, but we try.
Question nine
Anything we should know about how you eat, move, or rest?
Dietary notes, mobility, sleep, anything at all. Candor here makes the month better.
Question ten
What are you quietly hoping this month gives you?
Question eleven
And what do you think you might give the table, in return?
Not a pitch. Just what you tend to bring into a room full of people you are about to spend thirty nights with.
Question twelve · the last one
What does a long season mean to you?

Your application has been received.

We review each one personally. You will hear from us within seven days, by email, whether the answer is yes, not this time, or let's talk further. Thank you for taking the time to write with care.

Asante sana.

Thank you, deeply.
